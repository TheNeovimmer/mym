Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about MYM pricing.

Can I switch plans anytime?
Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade anytime. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades apply at the end of your billing cycle.

Yes. Both Plus and Pro include a 7-day free trial. Cancel anytime during the trial with no charge.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay/Google Pay through our mobile apps.
Can I get a refund?
Yes. If you are not satisfied within 30 days of your first paid subscription, contact us for a full refund.
Is there a student or family discount?
Yes. Students get 50% off Pro. Family plans (up to 5 members) are available at 40% off individual pricing.
