Sign of the Day

A new sign to learn every morning.

I Love You (ILY)

The ILY sign combines the ASL letters I, L, and Y. Extend your thumb, index finger, and pinky while folding your middle and ring fingers down. It is the most recognized sign worldwide.

FAQ

Everything you need to know about learning with MYM.

Do I need prior sign language experience?
No. MYM is built for complete beginners. Start with the basics and progress naturally through gamified lessons.
Is MYM free?
Yes. The free tier includes all core lessons and daily practice. Premium unlocks Mym AI conversations and offline mode.
ASL vs International Signs — what's the difference?
ASL is a full language with its own grammar, used in the US and Canada. International Signs is a simplified system for global deaf events.
How much daily practice is recommended?
5-10 minutes per day builds strong foundations. Our lessons are designed for consistency, not duration.
Can I learn on mobile?
Yes. MYM works on any device. The mobile app offers offline learning and push reminders.
